# Facilities, Library, Research & Governance — BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

## Library ("Ruby Block")
- Housed in a 4-storey dedicated building.
- Collection: textbooks, reference books, novels, encyclopedias, magazines, national/
  international journals — supporting teaching, research, and competitive-exam prep.
- Member of DELNET and the National Digital Library of India (NDLI).
- Catalogue organized via Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC); automated using the
  Engineering College Automation Package (ECAP), searchable via OPAC.
- Free access to AICTE's e-kumbh platform.
- Book Bank scheme for SC/ST students (extra books via Telangana Social Welfare Dept).
- Digital library: 10 internet-enabled computers.
- Remote access to subscribed e-resources (e-books, e-journals, IEEE-ASPP, J-Gate, previous
  question papers, lecture notes) via the KNIMBUS platform (self-registration with institute
  email); accessible on mobile via the "mLibrary" app.

## Hostel Capacity and Security

### Hostel Capacity
- On-campus hostel with **4 blocks**, **150+ rooms**, capacity for **500+ students**.
- Located within the campus for safety and convenience.
- Hostel facilities: common room, gym area, 24/7 water and geysers, TV/recreation area,
  nutritious food served on campus.

### Hostel Security Measures
- **Lady wardens** assigned per block, providing round-the-clock supervision.
- **Dedicated security officers** posted per block for safety monitoring.
- **Anti-ragging squad** actively monitors hostel premises and takes preventive action.
- Full-time lady physical director organizes sports and fitness activities.
- Campus-wide security infrastructure: CCTV, security guards at entry/exit points.
- All hostel activities are monitored to ensure a safe, supportive environment.
- Festivals are celebrated on campus; group study and extracurricular activities are encouraged.

## Campus Facilities (general)
- Food & cafeteria, gym, temple, on-campus security, hostel blocks, bus transportation.
- Sports complex maintained by the Physical Directress; hosts "NYPUNYA," a national-level
  sports meet held once every two years.

## Research & Innovation
- Active faculty patent filings across departments — examples include IoT-based traffic
  management systems, assistive tech (smart clothing for hand-fracture patients), wireless
  communication authentication models, satellite image classification, AI-driven road
  accident prediction, NLP for English–Telugu translation, and materials science
  (solid polymer electrolytes for batteries).
- Special centers: Vishnu Japan Outreach Centre (VJOC), VEDIC (Vishnu Educational
  Development and Innovation Centre), Vishnu Student Success Center (VSSC), WISE
  (Women in Software Engineering), Assistive Technology Lab (ATL), Graduate Study Abroad
  Center (GSAC), IoT Maker Space, Drone Technology Laboratory, Institution's Innovation
  Council (IIC BVRITH), Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC).
- Faculty are encouraged toward SWAYAM/NPTEL/MOOC certifications, conference/hackathon
  participation (Smart India Hackathon, GSoC, World Skill Competition, etc.).

## Governance & Committees (from institutional quality reports)
- College Academic Committee (CAC), Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC), Department
  Academic Committees, Finance Committee, Hostel Committee, Canteen Committee, Sports
  Committee, Co-Curricular Committee, Grievance Redressal Committee (students/staff/parents),
  Discipline Committee.
- Student feedback mechanisms exist for institutional governance and faculty performance.
- Grievance redressal mechanisms exist for faculty, staff, and students.
- Alumni are involved in Department Advisory Boards and IQAC, and contribute to placement
