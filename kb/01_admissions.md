## Seat Categories
- **Category A** (Convener quota): filled by TS EAMCET Convener based on TG EAPCET rank.
- **Category B** (Management quota): filled directly by the college as per TSCHE guidelines,
  application available on the college website once TSCHE issues the notification.

## Entrance Exams Accepted
- TG EAPCET (UG admissions)
- ECET (lateral entry)
- PGECET / GATE (PG admissions)
- College code: **BVRW** (EAMCET/ECET) | **BVRW1** (PGECET)

## Hostel
- On-campus hostel, 4 blocks, 150+ rooms, capacity 500+ students.
- Located within the campus for safety and convenience.
- Facilities: common room, gym area, 24/7 water and geysers, TV/recreation area, nutritious food.
- Lady wardens and dedicated security officers per block; anti-ragging squad actively monitors.
- Full-time lady physical director organizes sports (volleyball, badminton, throw-ball) and fitness activities.
- Environment supports group study and extracurricular pursuits (painting, dance, singing, etc.).
- Festivals are celebrated on campus to help students feel at home.

## Hostel Fee Structure
- Hostel fees at BVRIT Hyderabad cover accommodation, food (mess), and basic amenities.
- The exact hostel fee amount is revised each academic year and varies by room type (single/shared).
- For the current hostel fee, contact the admissions office directly:
  Dr. J. Manoj Kumar, Professor i/c Admissions — 92471 64714
  Email: info@bvrithyderabad.edu.in
- Hostel fee is separate from the academic tuition fee (₹1,20,000/year for 2022–2025 batches).
- Students must apply for hostel accommodation separately after securing admission.
- Hostel allocation is subject to availability; early application is recommended.
