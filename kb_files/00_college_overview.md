# BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women — Knowledge Base (Starter)

Source: https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/ (official website)

## Basic Facts
- Full name: BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women (BVRITH)
- Type: Women's engineering college, Autonomous
- Established: 2012
- Parent body: Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES), founded by Chairman Sri K.V. Vishnu Raju
- Affiliation: Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH)
- Approvals: AICTE, Government of Telangana
- Autonomous status: Granted UGC autonomous status from AY 2023-24 for 10 years
- Accreditation: NAAC Grade 'A' (2020, CGPA 3.23); NBA accredited for EEE, ECE, CSE (and IT accredited 2018)
- EAMCET/ECET College Code: BVRW | PGECET Code: BVRW1
