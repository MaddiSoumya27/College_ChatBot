## Principal
**Dr. K. V. N. Sunitha** — Principal, Professor (CSE), Ph.D. (Compilers)
- 29 years of experience; guided 14 Ph.D. scholars to completion, currently guiding 5 more;
  guided 20+ M.Tech and 50+ UG projects.
- 103 international papers, 15 national conference papers, 62 international conference papers.
- 7 patents filed (smart clothing for hand-fracture patients, real-time parking prediction,
  VR headset for visual-spatial learners, hand-tracking VR display, split-payment transaction
  system, road accident prediction device, coconut pulp extraction mechanism).
- Authored/edited multiple books on compilers, automata theory, and formal languages
  (publishers include Tata McGraw-Hill, Pearson India).
- Major grants: DST-TIDE (₹38.5L, assistive tech for visually impaired), DST-NIMS (₹30L),
  NRSC-funded satellite imagery research, Microsoft "AI for Earth" grant (precision agriculture).
- Awards include "Distinguished Principal Award" (CSI Mumbai, 2017), "Women in Education
  Award" (IPE, 2017), "Best Engineering College Principal in Telangana" (ISTE, 2019),
  "Acharya Ratna" national lifetime achievement award (2019).
- Professional fellowships: FIE, FIETE, Senior Member IEEE, Senior Member IACSIT.

## Note on completeness
This file was compiled from the mandatory AICTE disclosure PDF, which is long (65+ faculty
entries with publication/patent counts). The extract above captures department heads, the
Principal/Vice Principal, and a representative sample per department — not literally every
individual faculty member. For a complete, current faculty roster (all ~70+ names with full
qualification/publication details), fetch the source PDF directly via the crawler script or
re-fetch https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Mandatory-Disclosure-2026.pdf
(note the filename/year will change on future filings — search the site for "Mandatory
Disclosure" to find the current one).

Faculty professional bios (public, non-PII in the research/qualifications sense) are safe to
expose in a chatbot. Do not attach these details to student records or attempt to cross-link
faculty names with private contact info not published on the official site.
