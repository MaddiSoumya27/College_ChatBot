# Academic Departments — BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

## 1. Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
- Started in 2012 (UG); M.Tech CSE started 2024, intake 12.
- Research/focus areas: AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cloud & Fog Computing, IoT,
  Cybersecurity, Deep Learning, Image Processing, Information Security, Networks,
  AR/VR/MR, Blockchain, Parallel Processing, Human-Computer Interaction.
- Emphasis on state-of-the-art labs, strong industry collaboration, sponsored research/consultancy.
- Career paths highlighted: software development (front-end/back-end), network design &
  management, data analytics, software testing, systems maintenance.
- Has a dedicated TAP (Training and Placement) Cell support for interview/exam prep.
- Professional-body affiliations: ACM, CSI, and coding-practice platforms (e-Lab, Hackerrank,
  CodeChef, HackerBay-type initiatives).

## 2. CSE — Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (CSE-AIML)
- Focus: producing technocrats/researchers/entrepreneurs in AI & ML.
- Mission highlights: skills through learning methodologies/value-added courses, real-time
  exposure via internships/mentorships with industry collaborators.
- Department runs its own clubs/events (e.g., "Swetcha" club, AI Days, workshops on
  Agentic AI & Prompt Engineering with industry speakers).
- HOD: Dr. B. Lakshmi Praveena (Professor & HOD, CSE-AIML) — verify current HOD on site
  since leadership can change.

## 3. Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
- Seat capacity: 120.
- Focus: circuit design, embedded systems, telecom, signal processing, remote sensing.
- Career paths: telecom engineer, circuit design, process management, electronics engineer.
- Program Educational Objectives emphasize excellence in ECE problem-solving, ethical values,
  and continuous learning; Program Specific Outcomes include digital/signal-processing/
  communication system solutions and assistive tech for differently-abled users.

## 4. Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
- Seat capacity: 60.
- Focus: electronic circuit design/analysis across evolving technologies.
- Notable recruiters cited: Bosch, Capgemini, ADP, and other multinational firms.
- Strong historical placement record cited by the department.

## 5. Information Technology (IT)
- One of the four NBA-accredited UG branches (accredited 2018) alongside CSE, ECE, EEE.
- Runs its own FDPs/events (e.g., faculty development programs on AI, open-source tools).

## 6. Basic Sciences and Humanities (BS&H)

  common to all engineering branches.

## Academic Structure (BH23 regulations, current academic framework)
- UG program: 4 years / 8 semesters. Each academic year has 2 semesters of ~22 weeks
  (about 90 instructional days) with Continuous Internal Evaluation (CIE) + Semester End Exam.
- Honors program: available from AY 2025-26 onwards for eligible students (based on CGPA
  through II year I semester and NBA-accredited department preference); a student cannot
  pursue Honors and Minor simultaneously.
- MOOCs (SWAYAM, NPTEL, Coursera, edX, etc.) can count toward credit under Academic
  Council-approved conditions.
- Mandatory non-credit-heavy courses include Environmental Science, Constitution of India,
  Intellectual Property Rights, and Gender Sensitization lab.
