## Training & Placement (TAP) Cell
A dedicated cell trains students for aptitude tests, technical interviews, and HR rounds.
Department-level Placement Coordinators exist for each branch (ECE, CSE, CSE-AIML) who
liaise between the central TAP Cell and students.

## Important note for the chatbot
Detailed placement PDFs on the site (e.g. "2020-24 Placements.pdf") list individual
students' names, roll numbers, and packages. **Do not ingest or expose individual student
names/roll numbers in the chatbot** — that's personally identifiable information about
students who didn't consent to a public chatbot repeating their data. Use only the
**aggregate** statistics (company-wise totals, branch-wise totals, median/highest package,
placement %) for the knowledge base and chatbot answers.
